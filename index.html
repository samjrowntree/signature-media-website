About us

Signature Media is a successful and ever expanding publishing company, founded by Cathy Wagstaff in 2002. With the original idea of Holidays with Kids magazine which is now the leading family travel title both in reputation and readership, Signature Media now publish a range of leading travel titles including; Caravan & Camping with Kids, Ski & Snowboard with Kids, Signature Travel & Style, Signature Spa Beauty & Wellness and Five Star Kids.
